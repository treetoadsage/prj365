Project: ichi (meaning "one" in Japanese) is a big step towards Joseph Barnes career as a Software Developer. Inspired by "180 websites in 180 days" by Jennifer Dewalt and the now deleted YouTube channel "Unus Annus", hosted by Mark Fischbach (Markiplier) and Ethan Nestor (Crankgameplays), ichi will be a collection of 365 websites posted daily over the course of a year. The goal of ichi is to develop better habits and a portfolio of projects for building a strong foundation of skills.
